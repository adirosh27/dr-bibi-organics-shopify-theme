Return and Refund Policy

Dr. Bibi Organics

Thank you for choosing Dr. Bibi Organics! We hope you love your purchase, such as our Butter Oasis moisturizer, but if you're not fully satisfied, please contact us at support@drbibiorganics.com within 30 calendar days from the date your order was delivered.

Returns

  • You have 30 calendar days from the delivery date to return any item. After this period, we cannot offer returns, refunds, or exchanges.
  • To be eligible for a return, items must be received in their original packaging.
  • A receipt or proof of purchase is required to process your return.
  • Shipping fees are non-refundable.

Refunds

  • Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we'll send you an email to confirm we've received it.
  • If your return is approved, we'll process your refund within 1-2 business days.
  • Please allow 5-7 business days for the refund to reflect on your account.

Exchanges

We will only replace items that are damaged or defective, and only if reported within 30 calendar days from the delivery date.

Questions About Returns?

Contact us if you have any questions about our return and refund policy.
