Return & Refund Policy

Effective Date: December 8, 2024

30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee

We want you to love our products. If you're not satisfied, you can return Butter Oasis Balm within 30 days of delivery.

Refund Amount:

Product price minus $15 processing fee

(Original shipping costs are non-refundable)

How to Return

  1. Contact Us: Email Support@drbibiorganics.com within 30 days of delivery
  2. Provide Order Details: Include your order number and reason for return
  3. Get Return Authorization: We'll send you return instructions and a return authorization number
  4. Ship the Product: Send the product back in its original condition (at least 50% remaining)
  5. Receive Refund: Once we receive and inspect the return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days

Return Requirements

To be eligible for a return:

  • Product must be at least 50% full
  • Product must be in its original packaging
  • Return must be requested within 30 days of delivery
  • You must have proof of purchase (order number)
  • Product must not be damaged due to misuse

Return Shipping

  • Customers are responsible for return shipping costs
  • We recommend using a trackable shipping service
  • Dr. Bibi Organics is not responsible for items lost or damaged in return transit
  • Returns must be sent to the address provided in your return authorization

Refund Processing

Once your return is received and inspected:

  • We'll email you confirmation of receipt
  • Refund will be processed to your original payment method within 5-7 business days
  • Depending on your bank, it may take an additional 3-5 days to appear in your account
  • Refund = Product Price - $15 Processing Fee

Damaged or Defective Products

If you receive a damaged or defective product:

  • Contact us immediately at Support@drbibiorganics.com
  • Include photos of the damage
  • We'll send a replacement at no cost, OR
  • Provide a full refund including the $15 processing fee

Non-Returnable Items

  • Products with less than 50% remaining
  • Opened products without original packaging
  • Products purchased from unauthorized retailers
  • Returns requested after 30 days from delivery

Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you'd like a different product, please return the original for a refund and place a new order.

Questions?

If you have questions about returns or refunds:

Email: Support@drbibiorganics.com

Response Time: Within 24 hours
