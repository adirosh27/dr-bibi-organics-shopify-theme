Shipping Policy

Effective Date: December 8, 2024

Free Shipping on All Orders!

We offer complimentary shipping on all orders within the United States.

Processing Time

  • Orders placed Monday-Friday: Ship within 1-2 business days
  • Orders placed on weekends: Process on the next business day
  • Holiday orders: May take 2-3 business days during peak seasons

Business days are Monday through Friday, excluding federal holidays.

Delivery Time

Shipping Method Estimated Delivery Cost
Standard Shipping 5-7 business days FREE

Note: Delivery times are estimates and may vary during peak periods or due to carrier delays.

Shipping Locations

We currently ship to:

  • All 50 U.S. states
  • Washington, D.C.
  • Puerto Rico, Guam, and U.S. Virgin Islands (may have extended delivery times)

We do not currently ship to:

  • International addresses
  • P.O. Boxes or APO/FPO addresses

Order Tracking

Once your order ships:

  • You'll receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking number
  • Track your package through our carrier's website
  • Tracking updates may take 24 hours to appear

Shipping Address

Important: Please ensure your shipping address is correct.

  • We cannot change addresses once an order has shipped
  • Address corrections may result in delivery delays
  • Packages returned due to incorrect addresses may be subject to re-shipping fees

Lost or Stolen Packages

If your package shows as delivered but you didn't receive it:

  1. Check with neighbors or building management
  2. Verify the delivery address
  3. Wait 24 hours (sometimes marked delivered early)
  4. Contact us at Support@drbibiorganics.com

We'll work with you to resolve the issue. Replacement shipments may be sent after investigation.

Damaged Packages

If your package arrives damaged:

  • Take photos of the packaging and product
  • Contact us immediately at Support@drbibiorganics.com
  • We'll send a replacement at no additional cost

Weather Delays

Our products are sensitive to extreme temperatures. During very hot or cold weather, shipments may be delayed to protect product quality. We appreciate your patience!

Questions?

For shipping questions or concerns:

Email: Support@drbibiorganics.com

Response Time: Within 24 hours
