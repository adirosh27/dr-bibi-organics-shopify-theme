  Line Height: 1.5
  Usage: Main body copy, product descriptions

  Font: Inter Regular (400)
  Line Height: 1.5
  Usage: Secondary information, captions

Caption: 12px / 0.75rem
  Font: Inter Medium (500)
  Line Height: 1.4

### Typography Rules

1. **Emphasis Technique**: Use italic + semi-bold Cormorant for emphasized headline words
   - Example: "Deep healing. *Pure ingredients.*"

2. **Contrast**: Light weight for first part, semi-bold italic for emphasis

3. **Line Height**: Tight for headings (1.1-1.3), relaxed for body (1.5-1.6)

4. **Letter Spacing**: Default for body, tight tracking (-0.02em) for large headings

5. **Text Color Opacity**:
   - Primary text: 100% opacity
   - Secondary text: 70% opacity
   - Tertiary/captions: 60% opacity

## üñºÔ∏è Image Style Guide

### Photography Style

**Overall Aesthetic**:
- Warm, natural, organic, minimalist
- Inspired by: Aesop, Kinfolk, Cereal Magazine
- Avoid: Clinical/sterile, overly saturated, busy backgrounds

### Lighting

- **Golden Hour Preferred**: Soft, warm, diffused natural light
- **Color Temperature**: Warm tones (3000-4000K)
- **Direction**: Side lighting or soft overhead to create gentle shadows
- **Avoid**: Harsh direct light, cool fluorescent lighting, heavy shadows

### Composition

- **Negative Space**: 40-60% of frame should be empty/clean
- **Rule of Thirds**: Main subject off-center for sophistication
- **Depth**: Shallow depth of field for product shots (f/2.8-f/5.6)
- **Angles**: Slightly overhead (30-45¬∞) or straight-on for product shots

### Backgrounds

- **Primary**: Natural stone, marble, concrete, wood textures
- **Colors**: Cream, sand, warm white, natural beige
- **Texture**: Subtle, not distracting - complements product
- **Avoid**: Busy patterns, bright colors, artificial surfaces

### Product Photography Specifics

**Hero Product Shots**:
- 4oz jar as main subject
- Accompanied by 1-3 raw ingredient pieces (shea butter, cocoa pods, argan nuts)
- Natural surface (stone, marble, light wood)
- Soft shadows showing dimensionality
- Aspect ratio: 4:5 (portrait) or 1:1 (square)

**Lifestyle Shots**:
- Hands applying product (diverse skin tones)
- Natural, unpolished hands (real people, not hand models)
- In-context use (bathroom vanity, bedside table, natural settings)
- Aspect ratio: 4:3 or 16:9

**Ingredient Close-Ups**:
- Macro shots showing texture and purity
- Raw shea butter chunks, cocoa butter pieces, argan oil droplets
- High detail, rich texture, inviting tactile feel
- Aspect ratio: 1:1 or 4:5

**Before/After Comparisons**:
- Side-by-side or split screen
- Same lighting and angle for both
- Clear visible difference without heavy editing
- Real customer photos preferred over stock

### Image Treatment

- **Editing Style**: Minimal, natural, slight warmth boost
- **Saturation**: Slightly desaturated for premium feel (-5 to -10%)
- **Contrast**: Medium-low for soft, approachable aesthetic
- **Sharpness**: Moderate - clear but not hyper-sharp
- **Filters**: None - natural color grading only

## üé≠ Brand Voice & Copywriting Guidelines

### Brand Personality

**What We Are**:
- Clinical yet warm
- Premium but accessible
- Scientific and natural
- Confident without being pushy
- Empathetic to skin struggles
- Results-focused

**What We're Not**:
- Overly medical/cold
- Luxury/exclusive/gatekeeping
- Trendy/gimmicky
- Apologetic or tentative
- Generic wellness brand
- Fear-based marketing

### Tone of Voice

**Descriptors**:
- Warm authority
- Evidence-based reassurance
- Quiet confidence
- Empathetic expertise
- Understated luxury

**Writing Style**:
- Active voice preferred
- Short, punchy sentences mixed with flowing descriptions
- Second person ("you") for connection
- Contractions acceptable for warmth
- Technical terms explained simply

### Key Messaging Pillars

1. **Purity**: Only 3 ingredients, no fillers, no compromise
2. **Efficacy**: Clinical-grade results, deep cellular healing
3. **Heritage**: African-sourced, ethically traded ingredients
4. **Versatility**: One product, multiple uses, whole family
5. **Trust**: 30-day guarantee, transparent sourcing, real results

### Copywriting Formulas

**Headlines**:
```
[Emotional Benefit]. [Rational Proof].*
Example: "Deep healing. Pure ingredients."

[Problem] ‚Üí [Solution]
Example: "When ordinary moisturizers fail, Butter Oasis heals."

[Outcome]. [Method].*
Example: "Lasting relief. Just 3 ingredients."

* Use italic + bold for second part
```

**Product Descriptions**:
```
[What it does] + [How it works] + [Why it matters]

Example: "24-hour deep hydration (what) through cellular barrier repair (how)
so you wake up with soft, healed skin (why)."
```

**Benefit Statements**:
```
[Feature] ‚Üí [Benefit] ‚Üí [Emotional Outcome]

Example: "Raw shea butter (feature) rebuilds your skin barrier (benefit)
so you feel confident and comfortable (emotional)."
```

### Word Bank

**Use Frequently**:
- Transform, heal, repair, restore, nourish, penetrate
- Deep, pure, clinical-grade, lasting, proven
- Natural, organic, raw, unrefined, African-sourced
- Relief, comfort, confidence, radiance
- Gentle, safe, trusted, effective

**Use Sparingly**:
- Revolutionary, breakthrough, miracle, amazing
- Luxury, exclusive, premium (use "clinical-grade" instead)
- Anti-aging (use "restore youthful radiance")

**Avoid**:
- Cheap, discount, bargain
- Cure, medical claims
- Perfect, flawless, ideal
- Exotic, tribal (when describing African sourcing)

### Content Types

**Headlines**: Emotional hook + rational proof
**Subheadlines**: Expand on promise with specificity
**Body Copy**: Tell story, provide evidence, address objections
**Bullets**: Short, benefit-focused, parallel structure
**CTAs**: Action-oriented, value-driven ("Experience the transformation" not "Buy now")

## üì± Responsive Design Rules

### Mobile-First Approach

1. **Typography**: Reduce heading sizes by 30-40% on mobile
2. **Spacing**: Use smaller vertical padding (py-16 vs py-24)
3. **Grids**: Stack to single column below 768px
4. **Images**: Full-width on mobile, constrained on desktop
5. **Navigation**: Hamburger menu below 768px (md breakpoint)

### Touch Targets

- **Minimum Size**: 44px √ó 44px
- **Button Padding**: Generous for easy tapping
- **Spacing**: 8px minimum between clickable elements

### Performance

- **Images**: Lazy load below the fold
- **Fonts**: Preload critical fonts
- **CSS**: Tailwind purge for production
- **Scripts**: Defer non-critical JavaScript
